**ARMv8-A** is a [[microprocessor architecture]].

Adds 64-bit support.
Allows for a switch between two instruction sets:
- [[ARM32|AArch32]]
- [[ARM64|AArch64]]

It's tailored for [[application processor|application processors]]

Adds something called [[TrustZone]] for security.

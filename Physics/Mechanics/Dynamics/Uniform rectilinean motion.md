[[One dimensional motion of a point particle]] at a constant speed

${ v = \frac{dx}{dt} = v_{0} = const }$

${ a = \frac{dv}{dt} = \frac{d^{2}x}{dt^{2}} = 0 }$

**kinematic law**

${ x_{2}-\frac{x_{1}}{x_{0}} = v_{0}(t_{2}-t_{1}) }$
${ \implies \boxed{ x = v_{0}(t-t_{0})+x_{0} } }$ or ${ x = v_{0}t }$

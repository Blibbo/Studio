This operation takes a proposition ${ P }$ to another proposition "not ${ P }$", that means "${ P }$ is not true".
It's an unary [[logical connective]].

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Negation

## Notation

${ \neg P }$
${ \sim P }$
${ \overline{P} }$

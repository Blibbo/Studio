## Formally

Ordered pairs must have this property:

Let ${ (a_{1},b_{1}) }$ and ${ (a_{2},b_{2}) }$ be ordered pairs,
${ (a_{1},b_{1}) = (a_{2},b_{2}) \leftrightarrow a_{1} = a_{2} \land b_{1} = b_{2}}$

[[Kazimierz Kuratowski]] came up with the current definition:
${ (a,b)_{K}:= \{ \{ a \}, \{ a,b \} \} }$
